Still came about while I was working on the third album for my project ultradian. It started with three pieces where I wanted to move more towards ambient music with dense, deep soundscapes and long, narrative compositions using acoustic sounds. Putting both together is what made it interesting to me.

The name refers to the German word for quiet.
