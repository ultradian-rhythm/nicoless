How To Submit Your Music

I’m happy to receive your submission, but please make sure it meets the following requirements:

  • Must be Ambient, Contemporary Classical Music, Experimental, or Neo Folk/Indi
  • Instrumental or soft vocals (eg. Juliana Barwick, Hope Sandoval, Tiny Vipers)
  • A short list of words which describe the style (eg. melancholic, atmospheric, soundscape)
  • No singles (must be over 20 minutes)
  • No pre-release (must be fully available)
  • No streaming only (must be purchaseable)
  • Bandcamp prefered (feel free to send a short list of codes you want to be postet)

Ready? Then please send me a message through Bandcamp (no registration needed) with a link to your release and all the infos you want to share.
