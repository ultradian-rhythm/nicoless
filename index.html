New Website

I'm starting to think these blogs from 2004 worked better for artists than today's social media platforms. As a consumer, I don't want to follow artists and scroll through random postings. I don't need daily updates. I just want to spend some time now and then reading about what they're currently working on or getting an idea of who they are. So I thought maybe others feel the same and might want to explore the person behind the music, just for a while. Therefore, I redesigned my website into a very simple blog for infrequent posts about various topics.
